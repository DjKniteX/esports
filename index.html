SKYNITE Esports Club

Team SKYNITE is many things: An online collective, a gaming team, a streaming crew, and artists. With some of the like minded folks from Arkansas and around the world comes Arkansas's possible first and oldest Esports Club, SKYNITE. We plan on putting Arkansas on the Esports Map.

Nikki "DjKniteX" Xaysanasy

Esports Manager/Competitive Starcraft & Warcraft Player

DjKniteX has been following the Esports scene since 2007 with Starcraft and brought up the Esports Club to help promote local esports and compete in a variety of games. Aside from Starcraft and Warcraft you can expect to see him play in other games with other members in Street Fighter, Tekken and Pokemon TCG.

Kirsch "Skyryuu" Mackey

Competitive FGC Player

From dominating local arcades in his hometown in the Bahamas, he decided to take it to the next level shortly after moving to Arkansas. Known at parties and locally as the "One guy you don't want to play against" he decided to mark his return to competitive games by playing in Fightcade Weeklies and Planning his next trip to EVO 2020.

Logan "LtSturg" Strugill
